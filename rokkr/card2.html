How to join OnlyFans

Go to the OnlyFans website to create an account. You can create an account from scratch, or you can join with your Twitter or Google account. Once your account is created, you should be routed to your Account settings page.

 

From Account settings, you can change your username, email, and other preferences. The sidebar on the left lets you access your Profile, Security, and Notification settings.


Go to Profile to change your display name, set a subscription price, and more.
